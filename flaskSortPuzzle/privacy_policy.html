Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how kakenudo ("we", "our", or "the Company") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our mobile application Flask Sort Puzzle (the "Application").

1. Information Collection and Use

We do not require you to provide personal data (such as name or email) to use the Application. Game progress is stored locally on your device using Unity's PlayerPrefs system.

The Application uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify your device for advertising and analytical purposes:

3. Log Data

In the event of an error in the Application, we may collect technical information (device model, OS version, time of the error) through third-party products to help resolve technical issues.

4. Children's Privacy

Our services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we immediately delete this from our servers.

5. Security

We value your trust and strive to use commercially acceptable means of protecting technical data processed, but remember that no method of transmission over the internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure and reliable.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
