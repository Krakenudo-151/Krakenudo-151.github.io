2.2 Automatically collected information:

  • App usage data

2.3 Advertising information:

The app does NOT display automatic advertising. Ads are completely optional:

3.3 Legitimate Interest:

  • Improve and optimize our app through usage analytics
  • Prevent fraudulent activities

3.4 Legal Obligation:

  • Comply with legal obligations and respond to requests from competent authorities

All processors comply with security and privacy standards compatible with GDPR.

4.2 Special circumstances:

5. Your GDPR Rights

As a user, you have the following rights under the General Data Protection Regulation:

5.1 Fundamental rights:

  • Access (Art. 15): Check what personal data we store about you
  • Rectification (Art. 16): Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Erasure (Art. 17): Request deletion of your data (with 30-day grace period)
  • Portability (Art. 20): Export your data in JSON format from the app (Settings → Privacy → Download my data)
  • Objection (Art. 21): Object to processing based on legitimate interest
  • Withdraw consent (Art. 7.3): Revoke previously granted permissions
  • Not to be subject to automated decisions (Art. 22): The gamification system assigns points automatically, but does not affect essential rights or have legal consequences

5.2 Response timeframe:

We will respond to your requests within a maximum of 30 calendar days from receipt.

5.3 Account deletion:

When you request to delete your account, a 30-day grace period will begin during which you can cancel the deletion. After this period, all your personal data will be permanently deleted from our systems.

5.4 Complaint to authority:

You have the right to lodge a complaint with the Spanish Data Protection Agency (AEPD) if you believe that the processing of your data violates the GDPR: www.aepd.es

6. Data Security

We implement security measures including:

  • Data encryption
  • Firebase App Check
  • Secure authentication
  • Regular monitoring
  • Security updates

7. Minors

The app is designed for users over 13 years old:

  • Under 13 years: We do not permit use of the app. If we discover we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately.
  • Between 13 and 16 years: May use the app under supervision and consent of parents or legal guardians. Responsibility for appropriate use lies with guardians.
  • Over 16 years: May use the app without additional restrictions.

The developer does not actively verify users' age. It is the responsibility of parents and guardians to supervise minors' use.

We may update this policy occasionally. Modifications will take effect when published in the app.
